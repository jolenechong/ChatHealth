About Us

ChatHealth was created to bring help people struggling with mental health issues or even people struggling with managing their emotional/mental well-being in general. We do this by allowing users to chat freely with other users on this webapp , allowing users to rant anonymously when they are not comfortable talking to their loved ones about it, or also talk to others about topics of interest to boost their mood. There are also additional Challenges that users can take on to keep themselves occupied or even allow them to find new interests.

For more details and future updates, feel free to visit our github repo here
